How It Works
Paste link. Bot joins. Done.
No Chrome extension to install. No screen sharing. Just paste your meeting link.
Step 01
🔗
Paste your meeting link
Copy any Google Meet, Zoom, or Teams URL and paste it into MeetScribe. Add speaker names so the transcript knows who's talking.
Step 02
🤖
Bot joins automatically
Our server launches a bot that joins your meeting as "MeetScribe Bot". It listens to everyone — not just you — and captures all audio from the call.
Step 03
✨
Get transcript + AI summary
Words appear on your screen in real time. After the meeting, Claude AI generates a full summary with key decisions and action items.
